E-Learn

Role:UI/UX Designer

01-OVERVIEW

E-Learn Offers quality of learning and teaching. Meet the learning style or needs of students and Working professionals who are on a verge of a career change. Improves the Efficiency, Effectiveness, User-accessibility, and time flexibility to engage learners in the learning process.

02-PROBLEM STATEMENT

The App ensures the userâ€™s find Recommended courses based on their area of interest. E-Learn Study Buddy Feature allows students to connect with other students who are interested in studying together outside of class.

03-GOAL

This Project is about designing a native app for both iOS and Android which involves creating two versions of the app for user stories.
User stories and User Flow Diagram
  • 1. User able to sign in by creating a profile.
  • 2. User able to get Recommended course depending on the area of interest.
  • 3. User finding Study Buddy.

05-iOS VS ANDROID
